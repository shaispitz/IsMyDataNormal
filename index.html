Is My Data Normal?



KL Divergence measures the difference between your data's distribution and a normal distribution with the same mean and standard deviation.

Lower values indicate your data is closer to normal.

  • < 0.1: Very close to normal
  • 0.1 - 0.5: Reasonably normal
  • 0.5 - 1.0: Noticeable deviation
  • > 1.0: Strong deviation from normal
